## Key Learnings

- Infrastructure Management: Design, implementation, and maintenance of IT infrastructure components.

- Network Administration: Configuration and management of network devices, protocols, and services.

- System Security: Implementation of security measures and best practices across systems and networks.

- Service Management: Administration of critical IT services and ensuring high availability.

- Performance Monitoring: Implementation of monitoring solutions and performance optimization techniques.

- Problem Resolution: Advanced troubleshooting and incident management for complex IT environments.
