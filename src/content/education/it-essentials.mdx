## Key Learnings

- Computer Hardware: Assembly, maintenance, and troubleshooting of computer hardware components.

- Operating Systems: Installation and configuration of various operating systems.

- Network Connectivity: Basic network configuration and troubleshooting.

- Security Fundamentals: Implementation of basic security measures and best practices.

- IT Support Skills: Development of professional IT support and customer service skills.

- Mobile Devices: Understanding and support of mobile devices and their operating systems.

- Printer Configuration: Installation and troubleshooting of printing devices.
